Questions answered
Can I bring a plus-one?

Kindly check your personal invite — if plus-ones are included, it will be noted there. If you are unsure, do message us.

Are children welcome?

Immediate family children are most welcome. For the reception dinner, we kindly request that this be an adults-only affair.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes — hotel basement car park, $4/hr. We recommend arriving by 6 PM as it tends to fill up.

Any dietary requirements I should mention?

Yes please — let us know when you RSVP. Halal, vegetarian, and allergy-friendly options are all available.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We would love you to be fully present — kindly refrain from photos during the solemnisation itself. Snap away at the reception!
